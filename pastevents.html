The first dAppHack Berlin took place in November 2016. The first blockchain conference was devoted to decentralised data storage. It attracted more than 50 participants. IPFS, Secure Scuttlebutt, Bigchain DB, Oraclize, Parity client, DAT, Orbit DB presented their blockchain technologies.
