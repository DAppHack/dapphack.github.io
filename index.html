20-21 May 2017

DAppHack

Second practical unconference dAppHack Berlin will provide opportunity for the 80 blockchain developers to learn from each other how to use their latest technological advancements to create smart contract applications. Two day event will start on May 20 at 10:00 in Tempelhofer Ufer 17.

dAppHack mission is to foster development of blockchain technology by organizing events where developers meet to learn from each other to create decentralised technologies. Second unconference will be devoted to creation of smart contract applications. Organizers expect that at least 80 blockchain developers will take part in this event. Ethereum, BigChain DB, Monax, Oraclize, Ecotify will present their smart contract technologies.

dAppHack Berlin is two day event that is organized in a unconference format or participant-driven meeting, where any participant will have opportunity to run a workshop to present his/her developed technology and/or participate in workshops organized by other participants. dAppHack Berlin 2017 will be hosted by company builder The Next Big Thing in Tempelhofer Ufer 17. The unconference will start on May 20 at 10:00 and will end on May 21 at 19:00.

The first dAppHack Berlin took place in November 2016. The first blockchain conference was devoted to decentralised data storage. It attracted more than 50 participants. IPFS, Secure Scuttlebutt, Bigchain DB, Oraclize, Parity client, DAT, Orbit DB presented their blockchain technologies.

dAppHack Berlin is organized by Berlin based blockchain enthusiasts.
